Priority 1

Stain the back decking

Outdoor Anyone
Weather suitable.
Description

The back decking needs a full sand and stain before summer. Last treated three years ago — the wood is starting to look tired in places. Use the same oil as before but consider the darker shade this time.

Checklist — 2 of 6 done
Clear furniture from the decking
Sand the surface lightly
Clean with deck cleaner
Allow to dry for 24 hours
Apply first coat
Apply second coat (after 4 hours)
Shopping List
Wide paint brush
Already in the shed
Decking oil
2 × 2.5L tins
Sanding sheets (coarse)
Deck cleaner spray
Notes

Dry weather needed. Do not begin if rain is expected within 48 hours — the oil won't cure properly.
